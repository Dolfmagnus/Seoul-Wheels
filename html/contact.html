Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekend events. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests if our schedule allows.

What's included in the price?

Everything! Your flat rate includes unlimited food (all meats, sides, and banchan), all equipment (tables, grills, utensils), professional staff, setup, service throughout the event, and complete cleanup.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We can adjust our menu for gluten-free, vegetarian, and other dietary needs. Just let us know your requirements when booking and we'll customize your menu accordingly.

Do you provide indoor and outdoor service?

Absolutely! Our portable equipment works for both indoor and outdoor venues. We just need access to standard electrical outlets.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 72 hours notice for cancellations to receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours are subject to a 50% fee.

How long does service last?

Typically 2-3 hours, but we can extend or shorten based on your needs. We stay as long as your guests are eating!
