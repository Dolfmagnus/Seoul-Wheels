Our Mission

Creating Memorable Experiences Through Food

At Seoul Wheels, we're more than just a catering company. We're storytellers who use food to create moments that bring people together. Our mission is to share the joy and tradition of Korean BBQ with communities across Montana and beyond.

We believe that the best meals are interactive, communal, and filled with laughter. That's why we bring the authentic tabletop grilling experience to your location - so you and your guests can bond over the shared experience of cooking and eating together.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2019

The Beginning

Chef Kim Park started Seoul Wheels with a single portable grill and a dream to share authentic Korean BBQ with Montana. The first event was a friend's backyard party with 15 guests.

2020

Growing Demand

Word spread quickly about our unique mobile BBQ experience. We expanded our equipment and team to handle corporate events and weddings across the region.

2022

Award Recognition

Seoul Wheels was named "Best Mobile Caterer" by Montana Food Magazine and featured in regional publications for our innovative approach to event catering.

2024

Today & Beyond

We've catered over 500 events and served more than 10,000 guests. We continue to expand our service area while maintaining the quality and authenticity that defines us.
