Dietary Information

Accommodations & Notes

Gluten-Free Options

Many of our items can be prepared without soy sauce upon request. Unmarinated meats are naturally gluten-free.

Vegetarian Sides

All our banchan (except kimchi with fish sauce) can be prepared vegetarian. Fried rice available without meat.

Spice Levels

We can adjust spice levels for any dish. Just let us know your preference when booking.

Allergen Info

Contains: soy, sesame, garlic, shellfish (in some sauces). Please inform us of any allergies when booking.
